About PawPulse

PawPulse is a revolutionary pet tracking system designed to keep your furry friends safe by offering real-time GPS tracking, instant alerts, and health monitoring. With PawPulse, you can set safe zones and receive notifications if your pet wanders too far, track their activity levels to ensure their well-being, and even alert nearby veterinarians in case of emergencies.
