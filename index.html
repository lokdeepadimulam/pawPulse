About PawPulse

Welcome to PawPulse, Visakhapatnam's dedicated solution for pet safety and peace of mind! We provide a revolutionary pet tracking system designed to keep your furry friends safe by offering real-time GPS tracking right here in Vizag. Set safe zones, track activity levels, and ensure the well-being of your beloved companion with PawPulse.

What PawPulse Offers

Real-time GPS Tracking

Pinpoint your pet's location in Vizag with updates every 30 seconds.

Activity Monitoring

Track daily steps, active minutes, and rest periods for a healthy lifestyle.

Safe Zone Alerts

Define virtual fences (e.g., your home, park) and get notified if your pet leaves.
